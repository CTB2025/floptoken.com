About FLOP

FLOP Token is a comedic meme project featuring AI-driven price betting, letting you guess whether FLOP might flop—or secretly moon! With manual burns, buyback fees, XP rewards, and a lively community spirit, FLOP brings humor and crypto together in one degen-friendly package.

Key Features

  • AI-Driven Price Bets: Place wagers on FLOP price movements guided by AI predictions.
  • Manual Burn & Buyback: Community-fueled deflation that helps stabilize or pump the price.
  • XP Rewards: Earn XP for holding and betting, unlocking new perks.
  • Community Fun: Embrace comedic chaos—failures can still lead to success!

Roadmap

  1. Launch on BSC Mainnet – Deployed FLOP Token for everyone to see.
  2. AI Betting Platform – Roll out the betting dApp with AI-driven predictions.
  3. Partnerships & Listings – Expand on DEXes and form brand alliances.
