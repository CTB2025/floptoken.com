About FLOP

FLOP Token is a comedic meme project featuring AI-driven price betting, letting you guess whether FLOP or other tokens might flop—or secretly moon! We hope to build a robust community of users and encourage them to share stories on how much they have won or lost on other tokens, earn XP rewards, and enjoy a lively community spirit. FLOP merges humor and crypto together in one degen-friendly package.

Key Features

  • AI-Driven Price Bets: Place wagers on FLOP price movements with AI guidance.
  • Manual Burn & Buyback: Community-fueled deflation that helps stabilize or pump the price.
  • XP Rewards: Earn XP for holding and betting, unlocking new perks.
  • Community Fun: Embrace comedic chaos—failures can still lead to success!
