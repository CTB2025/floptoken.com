About FLOP

FLOP Token is a meme project featuring AI-driven price betting, letting you guess whether FLOP or other tokens might flop—or secretly moon! We hope to build a robust community of users and encourage them to share stories on how much they have won or lost on other tokens, earn XP rewards, and enjoy a lively community spirit. FLOP merges AI and crypto together in a user-friendly package.
